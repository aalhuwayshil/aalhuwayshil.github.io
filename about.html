I am a PhD researcher in Linguistics at the University of Edinburgh. My research focuses on scalar implicatures, cognitive pragmatics, and the interplay between experimental methods and formal semantics.

I’m especially interested in how language users derive meaning in both communicative and non-communicative contexts, and how this can inform theories of grammar, cognition, and artificial intelligence.
